Study Abroad

Finances

Study abroad costs depend on a number of factors, such as the duration and type of program, the cost of living in the program’s destination and when you go abroad. Below are resources that can help you feel more prepared while planning out your finances.

Some Ways To Pay For Study Abroad

  • Scholarships

Between those offered by universities, government organizations, not-for-profit enterprises, and other private foundations, there are thousands of grants and scholarships available. 

  • Crowdfunding

If you’re embarking on a shorter study abroad adventure such as an extracurricular summer program, crowdfunding is an option as well. Creating a campaign with a platforms such as FundMyTravel can be a great place to start.
